Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In the age of technology, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more important than ever. Our symptom-based disease diagnosis web application leverages Flask and Machine Learning to help users identify potential illnesses quickly and efficiently.

The Problem

Many people experience symptoms and seek immediate guidance about their health concerns. Differentiating between diseases can be difficult, especially when symptoms overlap.

The Solution

Our web application allows users to enter their symptoms and receive predictions powered by a trained Decision Tree Classifier.

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through a simple interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A trained classifier predicts the most likely disease.
  3. Prediction: Instant results provide insights into possible conditions.
  4. Additional Information: Users receive precautions, medications, diet advice, and workouts.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy navigation for all users.
  • Accurate Predictions: Machine learning-based results.
  • Comprehensive Information: Detailed healthcare guidance.
  • Educational Content: Blogs that promote health awareness.

Conclusion

This project combines machine learning with healthcare to make medical insights more accessible. By empowering users with knowledge, we aim to improve health awareness and support better decision-making.
